Data Explanation

1 Understanding the features

The world value survey is conducted in various waves and the data is often recorded in the format of coded variables that are representations of the questions that are asked to the surveyees. In order to extract information, we use the Integrated dictionary from the source to identify the relevant codes.

1.1 looking at the column names and their meaning

Code Recoding Variable Definition

S020 year Year survey

A165 trust_in_people Most people can be trusted
